Rent App

This app is to help people who normally struggle to find rooms to rent. The people we are targeting are students and Job seekers.

Students especially those who have to move because their school is far away. So then our app helps them to find rooms available closeby to the school premises.

Job seekers who just moved to a new area because of their current Job or looking for new jobs. We are also going to help them find rooms close to their workplace.
